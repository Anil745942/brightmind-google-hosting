About Gyanology

We believe that quality education should be free and accessible for every student in India — regardless of their background or location.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

Gyanology was created with one simple goal: make quality education free for every Indian student.

Whether you're a Class 9 student struggling with Newton's Laws, or a job seeker preparing for SSC CGL, or someone who simply wants to expand their general knowledge — Gyanology is here for you.

We write all our content in both English and Hindi so that language is never a barrier to learning.

Our Core Values

🆓

Free Education

All our content is completely free. No subscription, no paywall, no hidden charges. Education is a right, not a luxury.

✅

Accuracy First

Every article is carefully researched and written by subject matter experts. We prioritize accuracy over quantity.

🗣️

Bilingual Content

We write in both English and Hindi so students can learn in their most comfortable language.

🎯

Exam Focused

Content is specifically designed to help you clear school boards and competitive exams like SSC, UPSC, and Banking.

📱

Mobile First

We know most Indian students study on mobile phones. Our platform is fully optimized for all screen sizes.

🔄

Always Growing

New articles are added regularly. We keep up with current affairs, new exam patterns and updated syllabus.
