Privacy Policy

How BrightMind handles contact details, newsletter requests, cookies, advertising, and educational content.

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Overview

BrightMind provides free educational articles for school learning, competitive exam preparation, and general knowledge. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the terms that apply when you use this website.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily submit through contact and newsletter forms, including your name, email address, subject, and message. We do not ask for sensitive personal information such as government IDs, passwords, payment card numbers, or private school records.

Our server may also receive basic technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, referrer URL, and approximate location based on IP address. This helps us keep the website secure and improve user experience.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to questions, corrections, and topic requests.
  • To improve educational content, navigation, and website performance.
  • To prevent spam, abuse, security incidents, and technical errors.
  • To send newsletter updates only when a user chooses to subscribe.

Cookies and Advertising

BrightMind may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences and measure website performance. If Google AdSense or other Google advertising products are enabled, Google may use cookies to serve and measure ads based on user visits to this and other websites.

Users can manage ad personalization through Google Ads Settings and can control cookies in their browser settings. We do not sell personal information.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, spam prevention, email delivery, and advertising. These services process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Data Retention

Contact messages and newsletter records are kept only as long as needed for support, editorial review, legal compliance, security, or service improvement. Users may request deletion by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

BrightMind is an educational website that may be useful to students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent. Students should avoid sharing private personal details in contact forms.

Terms of Service

By using BrightMind, you agree to use the website for lawful educational purposes. You may read and share links to our articles, but you may not copy large portions of our content, misuse the website, attempt to disrupt our systems, or represent our content as your own.

We may update, remove, or correct content at any time. Continued use of the website after changes means you accept the updated terms.

Educational Disclaimer

BrightMind articles are created for learning and exam preparation support. While we aim for accuracy, educational material may become outdated or contain errors. Students should verify critical exam dates, syllabus changes, official notifications, and legal or government information from official sources.

BrightMind does not guarantee exam selection, marks, admissions, jobs, or financial outcomes.

Contact

For privacy questions, correction requests, or data deletion requests, use the contact page or email us directly at contact@yourdomain.com.
